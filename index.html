The FLUXNET Data Explorer

Explore and download a continuously refreshed (last updated: unavailable) compilation of the official FLUXNET Shuttle catalog, along with additional site data that are hosted elsewhere. The compilation includes observations contributed by regional networks worldwide, including data processed to the FLUXNET standard through the OneFlux pipeline.

Search by site ID, name or characteristic, then open hub-hosted download links directly from the table, or use the bulk download tools provided. Beyond the sites available through the FLUXNET Shuttle (https://data.fluxnet.org/), this explorer also surfaces additional records hosted on regional network portals worldwide, to provide the maximum amount of flux observations possible.

FLUXNET is an international “network of networks” that brings together over 2000 sites worldwide and thousands of scientists, educators, practitioners and policy makers. Using the eddy covariance technique, FLUXNET researchers measure exchanges of carbon, water, and energy between the land surface and the atmosphere. These data support research on ecosystem function, land–atmosphere interactions, and responses to environmental change.
